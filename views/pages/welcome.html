Tinkerers' Lab, IITH
Tinkerers' Lab is IITH's 24/7 open studio for exploration and innovation. It provides the tools for students to be creative and innovate.
Learn More

Our Aim

Innovation is the outcome of a habit, not a random act.
Here, at Tinkerers' Lab, our aim is to provide you with an open facility with a vast range of tools in order to bring out the inner tinkerer in you.

Always Available

We know that the best ideas don't always come to us during work hours. Tinkerers' Lab is open 24/7, giving you the liberty to build and innovate whenever.

Transparency of Ideas

Our Portal not only keeps a track of every single project in Tinkerers' Lab, it also has a section dedicated to sharing ideas, making collaboration and inspiration easy.

Resources

We have a wide variety of resources, from the commonplace RasPis and drills to CNC Machines and 3D Printers. You can also request advice from any proffessor directly from our portal.

TL Talks

From time to time, TL will host talks and sessions by innovators and entrepreneurs to help develop interest in new fields and push boundaries.
